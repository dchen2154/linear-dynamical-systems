Press C to enter the configurations tab. There, you will be able to set the matrix and intial state vector of the system. To move between fields, use UP, DOWN, LEFT, RIGHT, TAB, and ENTER. You may also utilize your mouse to jump to desired fields. To edit fields, you may type numbers and BACKSPACE/DELETE. Afterwards, press the indicated field to create the diagram. There, you may press RIGHT to play the next iteration or LEFT to return to a previous iteration.
